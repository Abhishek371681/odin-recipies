Pav Bhaji

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Bell peppers
  • Pav bhaji masala
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Pav (bread)
  • Butter

Recpie:

  1. Boil potatoes and mash them.
  2. Chop onions, tomatoes, and bell peppers.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Stir in pav bhaji masala and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Add the mashed potatoes and mix well.
  7. Adjust seasoning and add water for desired consistency.
  8. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  9. Serve hot with buttered pav and chopped onions.
