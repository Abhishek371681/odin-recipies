Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Butter or oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Milk or cream (optional)
  • Chopped herbs (optional)

Recipe:

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk them.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and let them set slightly before gently scrambling.
  4. Add salt, pepper, and optional milk or cream.
  5. Continue scrambling until eggs are cooked to your liking.
  6. Garnish with chopped herbs if desired and serve hot with toast or bread.
