Mutton Curry

Ingredients:

  • Mutton pieces
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Spices (coriander, cumin, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala)
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Cilantro (for garnish)

Recipe:

  1. Marinate mutton with yogurt and spices for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  3. Add garlic and ginger, cook for a minute.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add spices and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add the marinated mutton and cook until it's done.
  7. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.
